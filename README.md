## 🛠️ How It Works (কীভাবে কাজ করে?)
Start getting results in 3 easy steps. No complicated setup. No technical knowledge needed. Anyone can use SMMSun — just follow these simple steps:

### 1. Add Funds (Your Way)
Top up your SMMSun balance using bKash, Nagad, Rocket, Upay, or international options like Visa, Mastercard, Binance Pay, and Payeer. Whatever works for you in Bangladesh, we accept it.

### 2. Choose a Service
Browse hundreds of services for Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, and more. Select exactly what you need, enter your link, and set your quantity.

### 3. Watch Your Growth
Your order starts almost immediately. Track every order live from your dashboard. See real results — more followers, more views, more engagement.
