Refund Policy

What You Need to Know Before You Order

We know that handing over money to any online platform requires trust. Especially for first-time users who have never used an SMM panel before, the refund policy is often the last thing you check before deciding whether to place that first order.

So we are going to be completely straight with you here. This page explains exactly when SMMSun offers refunds, when we do not, and what you should do if something goes wrong with your order. No confusing legal language. No hidden clauses designed to avoid paying you back. Just a clear, honest explanation of how we handle money when things do not go as planned.

If you have questions about anything on this page, our support team is available 24/7 via WhatsApp and email. You can also visit our Contact Us page to reach us directly.

The Nature of SMM Services and Why Refunds Work Differently

Before we get into specifics, it helps to understand something fundamental about how SMM Panel services work. This context matters for understanding why our refund policy is structured the way it is.

When you place an order on SMMSun, whether for followers, likes, views, or any other service, the delivery process begins almost immediately. In most cases, your order enters the queue within seconds of being placed and starts delivering within minutes. This is fundamentally different from buying a physical product that sits in a warehouse waiting to be shipped.

Because delivery begins so quickly and the service is digital and non-reversible by nature, we cannot simply "cancel" a delivered order the way you might return a pair of shoes to a shop. Once followers have been delivered to an account or views have been registered on a video, those interactions have already occurred. They cannot be taken back.

This is not unique to SMMSun. It is how every legitimate SMM panel in the world operates. We explain it here because we believe you deserve to understand the nature of what you are purchasing before you spend your money, not after.

✅ When You Are Eligible for a Refund

SMMSun will process a refund or account credit in the following situations:

Your Order Was Never Delivered

If you placed a paid order and the service never started delivering within the expected timeframe stated in the package description, and our support team was unable to resolve the issue, you are eligible for a full refund of the amount paid for that order.

Partially Delivered & Cannot Complete

If your order started delivering but stopped before the full quantity was reached and our team cannot complete the remaining delivery, you are eligible for a partial refund proportional to the undelivered portion.

You Were Charged Incorrectly

If a technical error resulted in an incorrect charge to your account balance (e.g., charged twice or charged a higher amount), we will correct the error immediately and restore your account balance to the accurate amount.

Service Was Fundamentally Different

If the service you received was materially different from what the package description stated, contact our support team. We will review the case and issue a refund or credit if the discrepancy is confirmed.

❌ When Refunds Do Not Apply

We want to be equally clear about the situations where refunds are not available. Reading this section carefully will help avoid misunderstandings later.

Completed Orders

If your order was delivered in full according to the quantity and service type specified, a refund is not available simply because you changed your mind or are not satisfied with the results.

Drop in Counts After Delivery

Social media platforms periodically clean their systems and remove inactive accounts. If your count drops due to platform cleanup, this is not grounds for a refund. (This is why we offer refill guarantees).

Wrong Link or Username

If you entered an incorrect profile link or username and the service was delivered to the wrong account, a refund is not available. Targets cannot be changed once in the queue.

Private or Restricted Accounts

If your account was set to private or restricted during delivery, and the service could not be delivered as a result, a refund is not available. Accounts must remain public.

Deposited Account Balances

Funds added to your SMMSun account via bKash, Nagad, Visa, etc., are non-refundable as cash once deposited. They remain as a balance for future services.

Cancellation After Delivery Starts

If you request a cancellation after your order has already entered the delivery queue, a refund is not available for the portion already delivered.

Refill Guarantees — How They Work

Many packages on SMMSun come with a refill guarantee. This is separate from a refund.

A refill guarantee means that if your follower/like/view count drops within a specified number of days after delivery, you can request a free top-up to restore the count. The period is stated in the package description.

To request a refill, go to My Orders, find the order, and use the refill request option. We usually process these within 24 to 48 hours.

Guarantees apply only within the stated window. Once expired, the guarantee is no longer active.
